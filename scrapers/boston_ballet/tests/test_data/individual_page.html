About the Performance

Experience the magic of The Nutcracker with Boston Ballet this holiday season. This beloved holiday tradition captures the wonder and excitement of the season through spectacular sets, stunning costumes, and breathtaking performances.

Follow Clara on her magical journey through the Land of Sweets, accompanied by Tchaikovsky's iconic score performed live by the Boston Ballet Orchestra.

Perfect for audiences of all ages, The Nutcracker continues to be a cherished part of many families' holiday celebrations.
